# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Remove the "A Unit of Bhagyalaxmi Construction" sub-branding phrase from all user-facing surfaces, and add explicit instructions in the vendor selection step to choose "BHAGYALAXMI CONSTRUCTION".

**Planned changes:**
- Remove all occurrences of "A UNIT OF BHAGYALAXMI CONSTRUCTION" / "A unit of Bhagyalaxmi Construction" (and Hindi/Odia equivalents) from the page title (index.html), Header, Hero section, Footer, and all translation keys in all three languages (en/hi/od).
- Keep the standalone business name "Bhagyalaxmi Construction" wherever it currently appears.
- Update the SchemeInfo component's Phase 2 vendor/installer selection step in all three languages to explicitly instruct users to select "BHAGYALAXMI CONSTRUCTION" as the vendor, with no other vendor names mentioned.

**User-visible outcome:** Users will no longer see the "A Unit of Bhagyalaxmi Construction" sub-branding anywhere in the app, and the vendor selection step will clearly direct them to choose "BHAGYALAXMI CONSTRUCTION".
